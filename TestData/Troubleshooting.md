# Troubleshooting

## App Crashing on Launch
If the app crashes immediately, try clearing the cache or reinstalling. Ensure you are on the latest iOS version.

## Missing Data
If your data isn't showing up, check your internet connection and ensure you are logged into the correct account.

## Slow Performance
Performance issues can occur if background tasks are running. Close other heavy apps and restart.

## Battery Drain
We optimize for battery, but location services can use power. Check your privacy settings.

## Notification Issues
If you aren't receiving alerts, verify that notifications are enabled for this app in iOS Settings.
