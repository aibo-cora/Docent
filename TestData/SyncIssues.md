# Sync Issues

## Manual Sync
You can trigger a manual sync by pulling down on the home screen. This forces a refresh from the server.

## Syncing over Cellular
By default, large syncs happen on Wi-Fi. You can enable cellular sync in the app settings.

## Conflict Resolution
When two devices change the same item, we keep the most recent change. You can see history in the 'Activity' log.

## Storage Full
If your device storage is full, syncing will pause. Free up space to resume.

## Syncing with iCloud
Ensure iCloud Drive is enabled in your system settings for cross-device synchronization.
