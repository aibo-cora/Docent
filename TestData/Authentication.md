# Authentication

## Biometric Login
Enable FaceID or TouchID in the Security settings for faster access without typing your password.

## Resetting Forgotten Password
Tap 'Forgot Password' on the login screen. We will send a reset link to your registered email.

## Login Lockout
After 5 failed attempts, your account is locked for 15 minutes. Wait and try again or reset your password.

## Social Login Links
You can link your Google or Apple account for easier sign-in. Manage these links in Account Settings.

## Session Timeout
For security, sessions expire after 30 days of inactivity. You will be asked to log in again.
