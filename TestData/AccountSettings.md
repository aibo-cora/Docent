# Account Settings

## Changing Password
To change your password, go to Settings > Account > Security. You will need to enter your current password before choosing a new one.

## Two-Factor Authentication
Enable 2FA for extra security. We support app-based authenticators like Authy or Google Authenticator.

## Deleting Account
If you wish to delete your account, this action is permanent. All your data will be removed within 30 days.

## Updating Email
You can update your email address in the Profile section. A verification link will be sent to the new address.

## Managing Subscriptions
View and manage your active subscriptions under the Billing tab. You can cancel at any time.
