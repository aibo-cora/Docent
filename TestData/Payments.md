# Payments

## Supported Payment Methods
We accept Visa, Mastercard, and Apple Pay. We do not currently support PayPal.

## Refunding a Purchase
Refunds must be requested through Apple Support as we do not handle billing directly.

## Updating Credit Card
Update your payment info in the App Store settings. We pull billing data from your Apple ID.

## Invoice History
You can download your invoices from the 'Billing' section of our web dashboard.

## Failed Transactions
If a payment fails, check your card's expiration date or contact your bank.
